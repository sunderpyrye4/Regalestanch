What does the online consultation include?

The online format allows you to work with the interior easily and effectively, even if the client is in another country or has a busy schedule. The consultation is structured so that you receive maximum useful information at a time convenient for you.

It includes:

  • Analysis of the current interior: we will look at photos of the room and discuss the strengths and weaknesses of the layout.
  • Suggesting ideas for improvement: color schemes, options for arranging furniture, lighting, decor.
  • Selection of home decor elements: decorative accents, textiles, wall solutions, furniture combinations.
  • Answers to questions in real time: any nuance can be discussed immediately - from the texture of the curtains to the size of the sofa.
  • Mini-guide after consultation: structured information and recommendations so you can easily implement them.

This approach not only saves time, but also helps to avoid mistakes that often arise when choosing interior elements on your own.
