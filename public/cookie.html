Understanding Cookies and Their Classifications

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access a website. Their primary function is to improve your browsing experience by recalling details such as login credentials, preferences, and navigation pathways. For the purposes of this policy, cookies are categorized into distinct types:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are indispensable for the website's basic functionality. Without them, core services like security, network management, and accessibility would be compromised.
  • Performance Measurement Cookies: These collect data on how visitors interact with our site. They assist us in enhancing overall website performance by analyzing user behavior patterns and identifying areas for improvement.
  • Functionality Cookies: These enable our website to recall your selections (such as username, preferred language, or geographical region) and deliver an improved, customized content experience.
  • Targeted Advertising Cookies: These are utilized to present content that aligns with your specific interests. They can also help limit the frequency of advertisements you encounter and assess the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • Website Analytics Cookies: Analytics cookies help us compile aggregated, anonymous data regarding how visitors use our site. This information is used solely for statistical analysis to optimize the user experience.

We continuously monitor the origin and nature of cookies deployed on our website to ensure full adherence to all relevant privacy and data protection standards.

Information Gathered Through Cookies

Our website uses cookies to collect data that does not directly identify you personally. This information may encompass, but is not limited to:

  • User settings and site preferences
  • Type and version of browser
  • Operating system and device specifications
  • Origin of visit, such as the referring website or marketing initiative
  • Pages viewed and the duration of each visit
  • Interaction data, including clicks, mouse movements, and scrolling activity

While cookies allow us to recognize returning users, we do not employ them to gather sensitive personal information unless explicit consent has been granted. The primary application of collected data is to enhance internal operations, evaluate website performance, and refine our promotional strategies.

Why We Utilize Cookies

The core objective behind our use of cookies is to deliver a smooth, efficient, and engaging online experience for you. Specific reasons include:

  • Advertising and Promotional Activities: Through the use of targeting and advertising cookies, we aim to present you with advertisements that are relevant to your interests, facilitating a more personalized marketing approach.
  • Website Security and Fraud Prevention: Essential cookies play a vital role in maintaining the security and integrity of our website, serving as critical components in detecting and preventing fraudulent activities.
